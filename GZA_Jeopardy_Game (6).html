How to Play

  • Select any dollar value on the board to reveal a clue.
  • Type your answer — start with "What is..." or simply enter the key terms. Partial and close matches count!
  • A correct answer adds that dollar amount to your score.
  • A wrong answer earns a strike. Three strikes and you're out!
  • Clear the entire board to unlock Final Jeopardy — wager part of your winnings on one last question.
  • Top scores are saved to the Leaderboard. Can you take the #1 spot?
